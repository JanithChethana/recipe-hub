Appetizer Recipes

1. Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • French bread or Baguette
  • Butter (softened)
  • Minced garlic
  • Parsley (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix the softened butter with minced garlic and parsley.
  3. Slice the bread and spread the garlic butter on each slice.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes until golden and crispy.

2. Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Shredded cabbage and carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps:

  1. Sauté the vegetables with soy sauce until slightly soft.
  2. Place a spoonful of the mixture on a wrapper and roll it tightly.
  3. Seal the edges with a little water.
  4. Deep fry in hot oil until they turn golden brown.
