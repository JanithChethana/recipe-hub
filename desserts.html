1. Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar & Flour

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar, then combine with chocolate mixture.
  3. Fold in flour and pour into greased molds.
  4. Bake at 200Â°C for 12 minutes until edges are firm but center is soft.

2. Fruit Salad with Honey

Ingredients:

  • Mixed fruits (Mango, Banana, Pineapple)
  • Fresh Honey
  • Lime juice

Steps:

  1. Cut all fruits into small bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a small bowl, mix honey and lime juice.
  3. Drizzle the honey mixture over the fruits.
  4. Toss gently and serve chilled.
